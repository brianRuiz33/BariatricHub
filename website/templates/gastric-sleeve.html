Important Information

Risks & Considerations

Gastric sleeve surgery is a safe and commonly performed procedure, but like any surgical intervention, it carries potential risks.

  • ✔ Bleeding
  • ✔ Infection
  • ✔ Staple line leak
  • ✔ Acid reflux
  • ✔ Blood clots
  • ✔ Temporary nausea or dehydration

Every patient undergoes a comprehensive pre-operative evaluation to determine candidacy and reduce complications. During your consultation, your individual risk profile will be reviewed in detail.
