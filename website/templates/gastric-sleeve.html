Gastric Sleeve

An Endoscopic Gastric Sleeve is a newer, minimally invasive weight-loss procedure. During endoscopic sleeve gastroplasty, a suturing device is inserted through the throat and into the stomach.

The endoscopist places sutures inside the stomach to reduce its size, helping limit food intake and promote weight loss.

This procedure may be an option for patients with a body mass index (BMI) of 30 or higher who have not achieved results with diet and exercise alone.
