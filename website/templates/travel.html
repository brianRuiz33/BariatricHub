Travel Guide

1. Arrival at San Diego airport

The driver will pick you up and drop you off on the day of your arrival or departure regardless of when it is, as long as it is in the Hotel Zone we are not legally permitted to pick up outside of this area.

2. Transportation included

On the first day of arrival the driver will take you from the airport to the hotel, on the second and third day the operation and recovery in the hospital, on the fourth day the driver will take you back to the hotel and finally on the fifth day to the airport.

1. Extra nights

Our packages include 2 days in the hotel, the extra days in Tijuana generate an extra cost and are subject to availability (you must notify the coordinator as soon as possible; the coordinator indicates the extra hotel according to AC)

2. Recommended Hotel

Tijuana: Quartz Hotel & SPA Tijuana

Tijuana: Grand Hotel Tijuana

1. Includes 2 days in the Hospital

You will be able to stay in the hospital the day of the operation and 1 extra day for your recovery.

Please confirm your flight and hotel reservation to verify arrival times with the driver.

The US Department of State provides updated travel warnings to various countries.

There are no other travel restrictions for U.S. government employees in Baja California state. These include high-traffic tourism areas of border and coastal communities, such as Tijuana, Ensenada, and Rosarito.
